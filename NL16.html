Problems with CICD cluster

This last month of January we have been suffering a little in the keos-ci cluster (cluster where the Stratio CICD solution is hosted).

We currently have 5 physical machines that support the product workload, and we are waiting to see how we should expand this work capacity. Any instability of the service will be notified through official channels, but you can always count on information in the support channel (link below).

From CICD team we just wanted to tell you that we are working on it and that we hope to soon be able to give you news. In the meantime, thank you very much for your patience.
NFS y Tests de módulos en Keos_install

Como sabéis, hace unos meses unificamos los jobs de instalación de Keos en un solo job centralizado. Desde ahí hemos ido incluyendo nuevas funcionalidades para facilitar las instalaciones de Keos.

Hoy nos alegra anunciar que, fruto de un esfuerzo compartido con el quipo de QA (al que agradecemos desde aqui su dedicación), se han incluido las diferentes opciones de instalacion de Keos con NFS e IPD externo y la llamada a los tests de los módulos de una forma renovada y sencilla. Actualmente esta ejecución lanza uno a uno los modulos indicados.
En un futuro cercano se incluirán las paralelizaciones de primer y segundo nivel.

Esperamos que podáis hacer uso de esta nueva y renovada forma de lanzar las instalaciones y validaciones en los clusters. :)
NFS and module tests in Keos_install

As you know, a few months ago we unified the Keos installation jobs into a single centralized job. From there we have been including new functionalities to facilitate Keos installations.

Today we are happy to announce that, as a result of a shared effort with the QA team (whom we thank from here for their dedication), the different Keos installation options with NFS and external IPD and the call to the module tests have been included in a renewed and simple way. Currently this execution launches the indicated modules one by one.
In the near future the first and second level parallelizations will be included.

We hope that you can make use of this new and renewed way of launching the installations and validations in the clusters. :)
Política de limpieza new-docker-testing.

La semana pasada creamos una nueva política de limpieza en Nexus para purgar el repositorio new-docker-testing, que contiene milestones y prereleases de Docker. Esta política elimina todos los artefactos con una fecha de publicación anterior a 6 meses.

Si estáis utilizando una prerelease o milestone como imagen base en vuestros proyectos, tened en cuenta esto y actualizad vuestros Jenkinsfiles para usar una versión de release final.
Cleaning policy for new-docker-testing.

Last week we created a new cleanup policy in Nexus to purge the repository new-docker-testing, which contains docker milestones and prereleases. This policy removes all artifacts with a publish date older than 6 months.

If you are using a prerelase or milestone as buildtool image in your projects, now is a good time to update your Jenkinsfiles and start using a final release version.
